House Rules

Flat 1, 61 Caledonian Crescent

Respect & Noise

Please be considerate of housemates and neighbours. Keep noise to a minimum, especially after 10 pm, and respect others' quiet hours.

Shared Areas

Leave shared spaces such as kitchen, bathroom and hallway clean and tidy after use. Clean up spills promptly and store personal items neatly.

Guests

Overnight guests are allowed occasionally with prior agreement from all occupants. Long-term stays are not permitted without the lead tenant's consent.

Internet Use

Use the provided internet responsibly. Avoid illegal downloads or activities that may compromise the network. Do not share the Wi‑Fi password outside the household.

Smoking/Vaping

Smoking and vaping are not permitted inside the property. You may smoke or vape outside away from open windows and doors, and dispose of waste responsibly.

Pets

No pets are allowed on the premises without written permission from the lead tenant and managing agent.

Safety & Repairs

Report any maintenance issues or safety concerns to the lead tenant promptly. Do not attempt major repairs yourself.

Parking/Mail/Storage

Use designated parking spaces only. Collect your mail regularly and do not let parcels accumulate. Store bicycles and personal items in designated areas.
